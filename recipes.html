Green Pea Cutlet

Serving Size: 10

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 2-3 green chilis
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • Salt (to taste)
  • ½ tsp mango powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp cumin and coriander powder
  • ½ cup oil
  • ¼ cup finely chopped green coriander leaves
