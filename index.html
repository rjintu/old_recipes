Protecting your heart

The heart is one of the most important organs in the entire human body. It ensures that oxygenated blood passes throughout. Make sure to take care of it - your body will thank you!

Most of the time, our heart operates the way we want it to. Unfortunately, thousands of Americans will suffer from a heart attack each year.

According to the American Heart Association, a heart attack occurs when the heart does not receive enough blood to function.

About every 40 seconds, someone in the United States has a heart attack.

This website aims to provide information about the heart and ways to reduce the risk of a heart attack. The following pages contain a game, calendar tracker, an incentive program, and suggestions to live a happy, healthy life. Thanks for stopping by!
